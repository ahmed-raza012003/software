Frequently Asked Questions

You May Ask

What services does your software company offer?
We specialize in custom software development, mobile apps, web applications, and SaaS solutions tailored to your business needs.
Do you provide ongoing support after development?
Yes, we offer long-term support, maintenance, and updates for all software projects we develop.
How much time does it take to build a custom app?
Depending on complexity, a basic app may take 4–8 weeks, while complex systems could require 3–6 months.
Do you offer NDA and data privacy assurance?
Absolutely. We respect your data and can sign NDAs to ensure confidentiality and security.
Can you integrate payment gateways in my app?
Yes, we can integrate PayPal, Stripe, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and many other global/local payment gateways.
What technologies do you work with?
We work with Laravel, React, Node.js, Flutter, Vue.js, Python, MySQL, and more depending on your project requirements.
Do you redesign or upgrade existing apps?
Yes, we can enhance, redesign, or refactor your existing app to improve performance and design.
What is your project development process?
We follow a structured process: Requirement Gathering → UI/UX Design → Development → Testing → Deployment → Support.
Do you offer cross-platform mobile apps?
Yes, we build cross-platform apps using Flutter and React Native to run smoothly on Android and iOS.
How do I get started with my project?
You can get started by contacting us through our website or email. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs.
